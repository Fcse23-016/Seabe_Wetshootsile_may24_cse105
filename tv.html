Classic Releases

  • Looney Tunes was originally created to promote Warner Bros.’s music catalog in the 1930s, hence the name “Tunes” instead of “Toons”.
  • The series made its debut in 1930 with the release of the cartoon short “Sinkin’ in the Bathtub”.
  • The most iconic characters of Looney Tunes include Bugs Bunny, Daffy Duck, Porky Pig, Tweety bird and Sylvester the cat.

Ted Lasso is an American sports comedy-drama television series developed by Jason Sudeikis, Bill Lawrence, Brendan Hunt, and Joe Kelly, based on a character Sudeikis portrayed in a series of promotional media for NBC Sports's coverage of England's Premier League. The show follows Ted Lasso, an American college football coach who is hired to coach an English soccer team.
